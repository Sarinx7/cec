About the College

College of Engineering Chengannur (CEC) is one of the top engineering colleges in Kerala. It was started in 1993 and is managed by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD). The college is located in a peaceful and green campus in Chengannur, Alappuzha district.

CEC offers undergraduate programs in various engineering fields and has a good academic record. The college is known for its experienced teachers, good infrastructure, and active student life. It also promotes innovation, research, and extracurricular activities. Many students from CEC get placed in top companies and go on to pursue higher studies in reputed institutions in India and abroad.

The college encourages students to take part in technical fests, workshops, coding events, sports, and cultural programs. With a strong alumni network and supportive faculty, CEC helps students grow both professionally and personally.
